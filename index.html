About

Robotics, AI, and automation with a system-level mindset.

I am a Robotics Engineer passionate about creating technologies that connect the physical and digital worlds. What motivates me most is turning ideas into practical systems capable of solving real problems and delivering meaningful impact. Driven by curiosity, continuous learning, and a passion for innovation, I enjoy exploring new technologies and tackling challenging projects. I observed how industrial data is monitored and interpreted in control rooms, and how safety and reliability requirements shape automation systems in critical infrastructure. Automation Intern

Siemens Digital Industries

At Siemens Digital Industries, I gained hands-on experience in industrial automation. This experience strengthened my understanding of how automation systems are programmed, supervised, and connected in industrial environments.
